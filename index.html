About Lilliam

Local expertise. Global standards.

How It Works

Four steps. One signed deal.

I keep the process simple, clear, and on your timeline — not mine.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a free, no-pressure conversation about your goals, timeline, and budget — in English or Spanish, whatever's easier.

02

Personalized Strategy

I build a search or listing plan tailored to you — neighborhoods, comps, financing partners, marketing channels, the works.

03

Tour, Negotiate, Offer

Showings, counter-offers, inspections, appraisal — I'm by your side through every meeting, document, and decision.

04

Closing & Beyond

Signed, sealed, keys in hand. I stay your real estate contact long after the closing table — for the next move, or the next investment.
